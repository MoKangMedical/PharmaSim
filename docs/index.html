How It Works

From drug data to market prediction in four steps

1
Data Ingestion
Clinical trial results, epidemiology data, pricing information, and regulatory status are loaded into the simulation engine.
2
Agent Network
1,800 specialized AI agents form a social network. Each agent has unique attributes, preferences, and decision-making logic.
3
Simulation Run
Agents evaluate drugs across 28 sub-dimensions. Information diffuses through the network, shaping consensus and recommendations.
4
Market Prediction
Composite scores, adoption forecasts, and launch recommendations are generated, calibrated against historical drug launches.
