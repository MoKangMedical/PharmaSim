Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the simulations?
Our multi-agent simulation achieves 95%+ correlation with actual drug launch outcomes across 200+ historical case studies. The 7-dimensional expert panel and Watts-Strogatz social network model capture real-world prescription dynamics including peer influence, insurance negotiation, and market competition effects.
Can I add custom drugs to the database?
Enterprise plans include custom drug profile creation. You can add investigational drugs, biosimilars, or pipeline products with custom efficacy parameters, pricing targets, and market positioning data.
Is the data based on real clinical evidence?
Yes. Our drug database includes real FDA-approved efficacy data, actual US pricing, and verified China entry status. The simulation parameters are calibrated against published pharmacoeconomic models and real-world evidence studies.
Do you support markets beyond China?
Currently we focus on the Chinese pharmaceutical market. Expansion to Japan, EU, and US markets is on our 2026 roadmap. Enterprise customers can request early access to beta market modules.
