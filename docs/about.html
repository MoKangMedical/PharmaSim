About PharmaSim

Revolutionizing pharmaceutical market prediction through AI agent-based simulation — built for the complexity of China's healthcare landscape.

🎯 Our Mission

To replace intuition-driven drug launch decisions with data-driven, AI-powered market simulation. PharmaSim empowers pharmaceutical companies to predict drug adoption, pricing dynamics, and market success before investing billions in commercialization.

🔭 Our Vision

A world where every pharmaceutical decision — from R&D investment to market access strategy — is informed by comprehensive, unbiased AI simulation. We envision PharmaSim as the industry standard for drug lifecycle analysis across global markets.

What We're Building

  • 🧠
    1,800-Agent Simulation Engine — A heterogeneous network of doctor, patient, and expert AI agents that simulate real-world prescribing behavior, information diffusion, and clinical decision-making across the Chinese healthcare system.
  • 📊
    7-Dimension Scoring Framework — Each drug is evaluated across epidemiology, clinical evidence, market dynamics, pricing, pharmacology, pharmacoeconomics, and insurance access — with 28 weighted sub-dimensions providing granular insight.
  • 🔬
    Full Lifecycle Coverage — From target identification through Phase 1/2/3 trials to NMPA approval and post-launch market access. Our platform tracks drugs across every stage of the development pipeline.
  • 🇨🇳
    China-First Market Model — Purpose-built for the Chinese pharmaceutical market with NMPA regulatory pathways, NRDL reimbursement dynamics, volume-based procurement (VBP) impact, and region-specific epidemiological data.
  • ⚡
    Real-Time Analytics Dashboard — Interactive visualizations with dimension breakdowns, agent consensus maps, competitive comparisons, and therapeutic area performance — all powered by live simulation data.
