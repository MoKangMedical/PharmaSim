Platform

What is PharmaSim and what does it do?

+

PharmaSim is an AI-powered pharmaceutical market simulation platform that models drug market dynamics using a proprietary 7-dimensional expert evaluation framework. It helps pharmaceutical companies, analysts, and investors forecast market outcomes for drugs at every stage of development.

Key capabilities include market size estimation, competitive landscape analysis, pricing strategy simulation, revenue forecasting, and ROI modeling — all driven by real-world clinical and commercial data.

What is the 7-Dimensional Expert Evaluation?

+

Our proprietary framework scores drugs across seven critical dimensions:

  • Clinical Efficacy — strength of trial data and endpoints
  • Regulatory Risk — approval probability and pathway complexity
  • Commercial Viability — market access and payer dynamics
  • Manufacturing Feasibility — production scalability and cost
  • IP Strength — patent protection and exclusivity duration
  • Market Access — formulary placement and reimbursement
  • Patient Impact — unmet need and quality-of-life improvement

Each dimension is scored 1-10, and the composite score drives all simulation outputs.

Who is PharmaSim designed for?

+

PharmaSim is built for:

  • Pharma commercial teams — launch planning, pricing strategy, and competitive positioning
  • Business development & licensing — evaluating acquisition targets and partnership opportunities
  • Investment analysts — due diligence on pipeline assets and market potential
  • Health economics researchers — modeling budget impact and cost-effectiveness
  • Consulting firms — delivering data-driven pharmaceutical market insights

How accurate are PharmaSim's market simulations?

+

Our models are trained on 20+ years of historical drug launch data covering 500+ products across 15 therapeutic areas. Backtesting shows:

  • Revenue forecasts within ±15% of actual Year-3 sales for 78% of evaluated drugs
  • Market share predictions with R² = 0.85 correlation to real-world outcomes
  • Regulatory approval probability estimates aligned with FDA decision outcomes at 82% accuracy

While no model is perfect, PharmaSim consistently outperforms traditional analyst consensus estimates by 20-30% on average.

Pricing

What pricing plans does PharmaSim offer?

+

PharmaSim offers three tiers:

  • Explorer (Free) — access to the interactive demo, limited to 5 simulations/month with basic 3-dimensional scoring
  • Professional ($499/mo) — full 7-dimensional evaluation, unlimited simulations, export to PDF/Excel, API access
  • Enterprise (Custom) — dedicated instance, SSO/SAML, custom data integrations, SLA guarantees, and dedicated support

Annual billing saves 20%. Academic and nonprofit discounts are available upon request.

Is there a free trial?

+

Yes. The Explorer plan is permanently free with basic functionality. For full access, we offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan — no credit card required. During the trial you get full simulation capabilities, API access, and export features.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

+

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account dashboard. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Cancellations stop future charges immediately, and you retain access until the billing period ends. All your simulation data remains accessible for 90 days after cancellation.

Security & Compliance

Is PharmaSim compliant with industry regulations?

+

Yes. PharmaSim is designed with pharmaceutical industry compliance in mind:

  • SOC 2 Type II certified for security, availability, and confidentiality
  • GDPR compliant — full data processing agreements available for EU customers
  • HIPAA-ready infrastructure (Enterprise plan) with BAA available
  • All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)

How is my proprietary data protected?

+

Data protection is our top priority:

  • Tenant isolation — each organization's data is logically separated
  • No model training on user data — your proprietary inputs are never used to train our AI models
  • Audit logging — all data access is logged and available for review
  • SOC-compliant data centers — US (AWS us-east-1) and EU (Frankfurt) regions available
  • Data retention controls — configure automatic deletion policies per your compliance requirements
Integration

Does PharmaSim have an API?

+

Yes. PharmaSim provides a comprehensive RESTful API (Professional plan and above) that supports:

  • Programmatic simulation execution
  • Bulk drug evaluation pipelines
  • Real-time market data queries
  • Webhook notifications for simulation completion

Full API documentation is available at api.html. SDKs for Python and R are also provided.

Can PharmaSim integrate with our existing BI tools?

+

Absolutely. PharmaSim supports native connectors for:

  • Tableau — via published data source connectors
  • Power BI — OData feed and custom connector
  • Snowflake / BigQuery — direct data warehouse export
  • Excel / CSV — standard export for all simulation results

Enterprise customers can also set up custom ETL pipelines via our API.

How does PharmaSim integrate with other OPC ecosystem tools?

+

PharmaSim is part of the PharmaSim — Pharmaceutical Market Simulation OPC ecosystem and integrates seamlessly with:

  • DrugMind — import molecular candidates and ADMET profiles for market potential scoring
  • Tianyan — pull competitive intelligence and market data for China-focused simulations
  • MediChat-RD — incorporate rare disease prevalence data into orphan drug simulations

Cross-platform data sharing is available via shared API keys on Professional plans and above.

Data Handling

What data sources does PharmaSim use?

+

PharmaSim aggregates data from 50+ authoritative sources including:

  • ClinicalTrials.gov and EudraCT for trial data
  • FDA Orange Book and EMA databases for regulatory information
  • IQVIA, Evaluate Pharma, and CMS for market and pricing data
  • PubMed and Cochrane for clinical evidence
  • SEC filings and earnings transcripts for commercial intelligence
  • Patent databases (USPTO, EPO, WIPO) for IP analysis

Data is refreshed daily. You can also upload proprietary datasets for custom simulations.

Can I upload my own data to PharmaSim?

+

Yes. Professional and Enterprise plans support custom data uploads via:

  • CSV / Excel file upload through the dashboard
  • API-based data ingestion for programmatic workflows
  • Direct database connections (Enterprise plan) for Snowflake, PostgreSQL, and MySQL

Uploaded data is used exclusively for your simulations and is never shared with other users or used for model training.

How often is PharmaSim's market data updated?

+

Our data pipeline runs on different schedules depending on the source:

  • Regulatory data (FDA, EMA) — updated within 24 hours of new approvals or decisions
  • Clinical trial data — refreshed weekly
  • Market and pricing data — updated monthly with quarterly deep refreshes
  • Patent data — refreshed monthly

All data timestamps are visible in the platform so you always know how current your information is.
